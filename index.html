About Me

My name is Samira Vafay-Eslahi, and I will be joining the University of North Florida in Fall 2026 as an Assistant Professor in Electrical Engineering.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Texas A&M University in 2022.

After my Ph.D., I was a postdoc at Harvard Medical School and Massachusetts General Hospital (2022–2024).

In 2025, I joined Southern Illinois University Carbondale as Assistant Professor of Practice.

Research Focus

Medical imaging, signal processing, and hardware design.

Research Interest

My research focuses on medical imaging at the intersection of engineering and radiology.
